About this list

This page lists related AI tutoring, writing-support and education-prompt resources.

Some are managed education platforms. Some are general-purpose AI tools with tutoring features. Some are open prompt collections that can be inspected and adapted.

Inclusion does not mean endorsement. Use your own judgement, check privacy and assessment rules, and choose tools that support learning rather than replacing it.

How to read this list

These resources do different jobs. Some are managed platforms with dashboards, workflows and institutional controls. Some are general AI tools with project or custom-assistant features. Some are open prompt resources that can be inspected and adapted.

The AI Personal Tutor Toolkit is closest to the open prompt-resource category. Its main strengths are portability, inspectability, low cost, versioned files and explicit learning guardrails. It does not provide the dashboards, analytics, account management or compliance features of a managed institutional platform.
