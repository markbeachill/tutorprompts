User

This study show you how BTS and YouTubers advertising effect consumer culture, and how it increase audience engagement in digital media settings.

Chatbot

Paragraph 1

This study (1) show (2) you how (3) BTS and YouTubers advertising (4) effect consumer culture, and how (5) it increase audience engagement in digital media settings.

Mistake number Mistake in context Correction only Explanation Plain English grammar note
1 This study show shows “Study” is singular, so the verb needs -s. When one thing does the action, the verb often needs s: “the study shows”.
2 show you how remove “you” “You” sounds informal and directly addresses the reader. Academic writing usually avoids this unless the task allows it. In academic writing, avoid speaking directly to the reader unless required.
3 BTS and YouTubers advertising clarify the phrase The phrase is unclear. Do you mean advertising by BTS and YouTubers, advertising featuring them, or advertising about them? The reader needs to know the relationship between the people and the advertising.
4 advertising effect consumer culture affects “Effect” is usually a noun. Here you need the verb affects, meaning “has an influence on”. Affect is usually the action. Effect is usually the result.
5 how it increase audience engagement it increases / they increase The verb needs -s if “it” refers to “advertising”. But if “it” refers to BTS and YouTubers, use “they increase”. The verb must match the subject: “it increases” but “they increase”.

Summary table

ID Type of mistake Example Quantity
1 Subject-verb agreement “study show”, “it increase” 2
2 Clarity “BTS and YouTubers advertising” 1
3 Word choice “effect” instead of “affects” 1
4 Academic tone “you” 1

The mistake that most affects meaning is clarity, because the reader cannot yet tell exactly how BTS, YouTubers and advertising are connected.

Which mistake type would you like to practise first? I recommend starting with subject-verb agreement or clarity, because fixing those will improve your writing fastest.
